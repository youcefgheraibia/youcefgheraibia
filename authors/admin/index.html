Youcef Gheraibia

Research Associate

University of Hull

An award-winning researcher (National Innovation Award) with over 10 years of experience. Currently working as a Research Associate in the EU H2020 funded DEIS (Dependability Engineering Innovation for Cyber Physical Systems) project at the University of Hull. I received my Phd in Data processing from the University of Annaba (Algeria) in 2016.

I’m particularly interested in artificial intelligence for road safety and logistics including machine learning, optimization, data analysis and Model-based Safety Assessment of safety critical systems. Over the years, I have had the chance to expose myself to different tools and techniques. I proposed a new concepts of optimisation using the Penguin’s collaborative hunting behavior. This new concepts was applied to different domains such as : logistics (VRP), Safety and reliability, Bio-Informatics and medical informatics, Social medias and geological system.
